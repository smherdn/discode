About Discode

Discode is a community that started at May 2022 for presenting a respectful and entertaining environment for developers and other content creators (designers, musicians much more). Also it helps people to find shared codes, documents, designs and other assets. It has an official YouTube Channel and a Twitter Account by now. By July 2022, it supports HTML 5, CSS 3, JavaScript, Discord.js, Java, C, C#, C++, SQL, PHP and Python 3.
